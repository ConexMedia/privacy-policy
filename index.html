2. Data We Collect

When you interact with the Service, we may collect the following personal data:

  • Telegram User Data: Your Telegram username, user ID, and profile picture (if available).
  • Messages: The content of your messages, including text, photos, and other files you send to the bot.
  • Chat History: A limited history of your conversations (e.g., the last 5 messages) stored to improve interaction quality.
  • Metadata: Information such as timestamps and message IDs for operational purposes.

We do not collect sensitive personal data unless explicitly provided by you during your interaction with the bot.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is processed for the following purposes:

  • To provide chatbot services, including generating responses and personalizing interactions.
  • To improve the Service’s functionality, including troubleshooting and development.
  • To send images or other media upon request.
  • To ensure compliance with applicable laws and prevent misuse of the Service.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Consent: By using the Service, you consent to the processing of your data for chatbot interactions.
  • Legitimate Interest: To improve and maintain the Service’s quality and functionality.

You may withdraw your consent at any time by discontinuing your use of the Service or requesting deletion of your data (see Section 8).

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties except in the following cases:

  • Service Providers: We use third-party services such as OpenAI (for GPT-based responses) and MongoDB (for data storage). These providers process data on our behalf and comply with GDPR.
  • Legal Obligations: If required by law, we may disclose your data to authorities or law enforcement agencies.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy:

  • Chat History: A limited chat history (e.g., last 5 messages) is stored temporarily and may be retained for a maximum of 90 days.
  • Message Logs: Messages and metadata are stored for up to 90 days to improve service quality and resolve issues.

You can request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 8).

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • Secure storage using MongoDB with access controls.
  • Encrypted communication with OpenAI APIs.
  • Regular monitoring and updates to ensure data security.

However, no system is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

8. Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request the deletion of your data (“right to be forgotten”).
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing under certain conditions.
  • Objection: Object to data processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a structured, commonly used format.
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw your consent to data processing at any time.

To exercise these rights, contact us at conexmediacorp@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Data Transfers

Data processed by OpenAI or stored in MongoDB may be transferred outside the European Economic Area (EEA). We ensure that such transfers comply with GDPR by using providers with adequate safeguards (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses).
