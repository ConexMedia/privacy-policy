Privacy Policy

Welcome to Bethildabot, operated by ClonexAI. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable data protection laws worldwide. Please note that this service is strictly for users 18 years of age or older.

1. Data We Collect

When you interact with Bethildabot, we collect and process the following data:

  • User Information: Your Telegram username, user ID, first name (if provided), and messages.
  • Payment Data: Payment details processed via Stripe for minute purchases. We store only transaction records, not payment card details.
  • Chat History: Messages, voice messages (transcribed), photos, videos, and other media you share.
  • Usage Data: Session times, activity timestamps, and minute balance information.
  • Voice Data: Voice messages are processed through OpenAI's Whisper for transcription and ElevenLabs for voice responses.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to provide and improve Bethildabot services:

  • Generating personalized AI responses using OpenAI's technology.
  • Processing and managing your minute balance and payments.
  • Tracking session times and activity for billing purposes.
  • Storing conversation history to maintain context and improve responses.
  • Converting voice messages to text and generating voice responses.
  • Detecting and preventing fraudulent activities.

3. Payment Information

Payments are processed securely through Stripe:

  • Available packages: $10 (10 min), $20 (25 min), $50 (75 min), $100 (170 min).
  • All payments are non-refundable as they pertain to digital services.
  • New users receive 30 free minutes upon registration.
  • Payment processing is handled entirely by Stripe; we never see or store your card details.

4. Data Storage

We use MongoDB to store your data securely:

  • User profiles and preferences
  • Message history and interaction data
  • Payment records and minute balances
  • Session and activity timestamps

5. Data Retention

We retain your data as follows:

  • Chat history: Stored for the duration of your account activity
  • Payment records: Kept for legal compliance and accounting purposes
  • Usage data: Maintained for service improvement and security
  • Voice messages: Transcribed and stored as text; original audio files are not retained

6. Your Rights

Under GDPR and other applicable laws, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request data deletion
  • Withdraw consent to data processing
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Lodge complaints with relevant authorities

7. Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Telegram - Bot platform and messaging
  • Stripe - Payment processing
  • MongoDB - Data storage
  • OpenAI - AI responses and voice transcription
  • ElevenLabs - Voice generation

8. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures:

  • Encrypted data storage and transmission
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Strict access controls and authentication
  • Automated threat detection and prevention
  • Regular backups and disaster recovery procedures

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is strictly intended for users who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect or store data from individuals under 18 years of age. If we discover we have collected personal data from someone under 18, we will promptly delete it. By using our service, you confirm that you are at least 18 years old.
