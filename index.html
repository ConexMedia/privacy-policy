Privacy Policy

Welcome to Bethildabot, operated by ClonexAI. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable data protection laws worldwide.

1. Data We Collect

When you interact with Bethildabot, we may collect the following data:

  • User Information: Your Telegram username, user ID, and messages.
  • Payment Data: Payment details provided via Stripe for purchases. Note that we do not store payment card details; Stripe handles this information securely.

  • Metadata: Non-identifiable data such as timestamps of messages and interactions.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to provide, improve, and personalize the services offered by Bethildabot. Specifically, we use your data for:

  • Generating responses and enhancing interaction quality.

3. Payment Information

Payments for credits or services are processed securely through Stripe. Please note that:

  • All payments are non-refundable, as they pertain to digital goods and services.
  • Your payment details are processed directly by Stripe and are not stored on our servers.

4. Data Storage
